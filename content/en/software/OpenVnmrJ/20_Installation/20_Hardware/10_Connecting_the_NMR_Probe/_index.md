The connector to connect the NMR probe to the Bridge12 SCN spectrometer is located on the front panel. The input impedance is 50 &ohm;.

If your probe has a BNC connector please use a [SMA-BNC Adpater](https://www.minicircuits.com/WebStore/dashboard.html?model=SM-BM50%2B) and a BNC cable to connect the probe.
