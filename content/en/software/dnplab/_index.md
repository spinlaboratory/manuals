[DNPLab](http://dnplab.net/) is an object-oriented [Open Source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_source) Python-based package for importing, processing, and analyzing data recorded in a [Dynamic Nuclear Polarization (DNP)](https://www.bridge12.com/learn/dnp-spectroscopy/) experiment. The aim of the project is to provide a free, turn-key python-based processing package DNP-NMR data.

DNPLab is a collaborative project created by:

* [Bridge12 Magnetic Resonance LLC](https://www.bridge12.com/)
* The Han Lab at [Northwestern University](https://chemistry.northwestern.edu/people/core-faculty/profiles/songi-han.html)
* The [Franck Lab at Syracuse University](https://jmfrancklab.github.io/)

DNPLab is distributed free of charge under the [MIT License](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MIT_License).
