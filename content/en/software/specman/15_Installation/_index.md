## Software Installation ##
Typically, your Bridge12 EPR spectrometer comes pre-installed with SpecMan4EPR and the system is already configured depending on your needs and the available hardware. This includes all hardware drivers and any additional software that is required to operate the spectrometer.

SpecMan4EPR is not free and a licensed is required to run the software. This license file depends on the computer hardware and can not just simply transferred to a new computer. If you change the hardware, you may have to obtain a new license file to activate the software. To bet a new license file, please follow the instructions in the [Troubleshooting Section](/software/specman/90_troubleshooting/specman4epr/#re-installing-specman4epr-on-new-hardware)
