## Unpacking the Probe ##

The QLP EPR probe is shipped in a sturdy case to protect the probe from damages during transportation (see picture below). Together with the probe several accessories are shipped. For example, sample sticks with sample holders, an additional 4 inch straight waveguide section (WR-28) as well as a 12 inch long flexible waveguide.

<center>
{{< figure src="/manuals/images/probes/bridge12-qlp/qlp-probe-case.png" caption="QLP and accessories in shipping case." width="600px">}}
</center>

To install the probe, simple remove the probe from the shipping case and insert the probe into the cryostat. The probe is compatible with the integrated VTI of the Compact-Q system and the Oxford Instruments CF935 cryostat.

## Storing the Probe ##

If the probe is not used, we highly recommend storing the probe in the original shipping case. This will protect the probe from dust and prevent any damages.
